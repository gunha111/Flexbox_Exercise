Japanese Ramen Ingredients

Noodles

Made from wheat flour, water, and kansui, these noodles are the base of any ramen bowl.

Broth

Flavors include shoyu (soy sauce), miso, shio (salt), or tonkotsu (pork bone).

Chashu

Braised or roasted pork slices that melt in your mouth.

Menma

Fermented bamboo shoots, adding a crunchy texture.

Ajitsuke Tamago

Marinated soft-boiled eggs with a flavorful yolk.

Nori

Sheets of dried seaweed for added umami and texture.
